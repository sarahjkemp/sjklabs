What this audit looks for

If the market cannot understand you quickly, it is harder to trust you, remember you, or retrieve you when it matters.

This audit is designed to show whether the public story is carrying the strength of the business properly, or whether your authority is still getting lost somewhere between what you know internally and what the market can actually hold onto.
