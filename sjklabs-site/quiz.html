What this quiz is actually measuring

SJK Labs works on the gap between real expertise and market understanding. For individuals, that usually shows up as under-positioned experience, weak public proof or the wrong authority channel. For businesses, it shows up as fuzzy positioning, inconsistent proof, low retrieval and a trust signal that is weaker than the work itself.

This page exists to give visitors a clearer first step, not just a score. If the result points to a bigger problem with narrative clarity, retrieval or authority structure, the next move is usually The Scriptwriter Test™ or AI Visibility & Narrative Audit rather than more random content output.
