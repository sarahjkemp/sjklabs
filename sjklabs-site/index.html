The Scriptwriter Test™

Most businesses are failing it. Here's why.

01

The Protagonist

Are you the Hero or the Guide?

Stop talking about yourself. Start talking about your customer's win. The market doesn't choose the most capable option — it chooses the one that makes them feel most understood.

02

The Stakes

What happens if the movie ends now?

Identify the specific cost of your customer's inaction. Not vague risk. Actual consequence. The businesses that earn trust make the cost of standing still impossible to ignore.

03

The Dialogue

Do you sound like a human or a manual?

Replace corporate jargon with an authentic Director's Cut voice. If your copy sounds like it was written by a committee, it probably was. Real authority sounds like a person.

In a world of AI-generated noise, Human Authority is the only thing that can't be automated.

I founded SJK Labs to solve a problem I kept running into across twenty years of working in marketing and communications — companies and people with genuinely excellent expertise who the market hadn't caught up with yet.

Most personal branding advice tells you to post more, niche down, and pick a colour palette. Businesses get the corporate version of the same problem — frameworks, pillars, and positioning statements that sound impressive and say nothing. This is different. I'm a journalist and narrative strategist who interviews people for a living. I read your documents, dig into your background, and find the story you've been sitting on. That process led me to develop The Scriptwriter Test™ — a framework for diagnosing exactly why your authority isn't landing, and what needs to change.

I trained as a journalist, and have spent years interviewing people across music, culture, food and business — including Blur, Tim Minchin and Adrian Chiles. My work, both as a PR professional and journalist, has been featured across all UK national titles and internationally in Bloomberg, Nikkei and Dazed & Confused. My podcast hit the Apple Music top 10. I was a semi-finalist on Britain's Best Dish, which taught me that following recipes is not really my thing.

I host PRception — a series that explores how attention becomes authority across markets, culture and real life — and I built Not Built For This, the product arm of SJK Labs: low-friction digital tools for neurodivergent and high-functioning overwhelmed people.

I'm a qualified fitness instructor and a certified spin instructor. I've done a standup comedy course and performed it. I did a singing course and performed that too. I founded a day club in Brighton. And I've spent more hours than I can count watching live music. None of this was accidental — I wanted to understand, from the inside, how attention is earned and held in real time. The same principles that keep a room don't leave when the lights go up. They're the ones that make a brand get chosen.

I'm neurodiverse. The pattern recognition, the systems thinking, the refusal to accept that things have to be done the way they've always been done — that's not incidental to the work. It is the work.
