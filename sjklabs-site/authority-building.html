What's in scope, and what isn't

Authority Building covers the editorial and earned-media work that builds external corroboration: PR, podcast pitching and bookings, journalist-led video interviews, ghostwritten long-form articles, founder visibility on LinkedIn and Substack.

It does not include website development, schema markup or other technical GEO implementation. That work sits with a specialist technical partner — recommended on request, contracted separately. This separation is deliberate. Editorial and technical are different crafts. Bundling them produces worse work in both.

What this is not

  • Not website development.
  • Not schema markup or technical GEO implementation.
  • Not a one-month publicity burst.
  • Not generalist marketing support for every channel.
